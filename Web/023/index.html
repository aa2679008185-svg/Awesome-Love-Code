my love word

Some people say that love can cost one's life, but I won't let that happen. I'll keep this life to wipe the crumbs from the corner of your mouth; I'll keep this life to buy you the roses you like; I'll keep this life to wipe away the tears from the corner of your eyes; I'll keep this life to build your happy paradise; if one day you find the one in your heart, I'll climb up the cliff to pick the flower in your heart for you! I want to live a simple life with you... In the morning, I can see your smiling face and the milk and eggs on the table, then we'll go out together, work hard, go to the supermarket to buy vegetables after work, go home together to cook dinner, and take a walk hand in hand under the setting sun... until we grow old.
